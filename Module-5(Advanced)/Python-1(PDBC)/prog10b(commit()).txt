commit()
-----------
1) What  does  commit()  method  do ?  --->  Males  changes  permanent

2) In  other  words, insertion , deletion  and  modification  made  to  the  database  should  be  committed

3) Are  changes  made  reflected  to  database  without  commit() ?  --->  No

4) commit()  is  mandatory  soon  after  changes  are  made  to  the  database

5) Insertion , deletion   and  modification  do  not  happen  unless  committed

6) Where  is  commit()  method  defined  ?  ---> In  MySqlConnection  class
									     Eg:  con . commit()
